How to use this Directory
 
How do I find a company?
There are two ways, one just scroll down through the list until you find the company you are looking for. Two click on the category of company in the Table of Contents and you will be directed to the companies listed in that category.
How do I find a local company?
All listings are listed under category first, next by location, then alphabetically.
My company isn’t listed in the directory, how do I get it included?

There are two ways, the easiest way is to go to www.oildirectory.com and click on “Add New Listing” under the Submit Listing category on the left side. This will open a form that you can fill out, once your company is listed on www.oildirectory.com it will automatically be listed in all future electronic phone directories.

The second way is to click on the "Contact Us" link above and contact one of our sales persons.
I would like to change something in my listing, how do I do that?

Again, the easiest way is to go to www.oildirectory.com , use the "Update an Existing" link on the Submit Listing category on the left side.

If it’s not possible to get to the internet, just contact us by the other methods in the "Contact Us" link and we can do the changes for you.
