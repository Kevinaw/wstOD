The gravel is swept up and
removed from the roof then an an
air compressor is used to blow all
the loose sand and dust off
Loose flashing is fastened down
and any place that won't be
sprayed is masked off.
The old tar and gravel layers are
cored and vents are installed (the
round spot right of the skylight)
that will vent moisture out of the
roof cavity. Then a layer of foam
is sprayed over all the flashing.
